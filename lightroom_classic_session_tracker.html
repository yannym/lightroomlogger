How it works:

Pic-Time relies on Zapier for third-party client sync. By configuring a **Zapier Catch Hook URL** below, our tracker will automatically request Pic-Time to generate new client galleries inside your platform matching your Lightroom Catalog structures!

Configure your Zapier Trigger as "Webhooks by Zapier > Catch Hook" and direct the action to "Pic-Time > Create New Gallery".
